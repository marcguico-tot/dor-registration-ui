General requirements

  • Register with ATF and the tobacco tax administrators of each state and/or locality into which shipments are made.
  • File monthly reports with the tobacco tax administrators of each state and/or locality shipped to in the previous calendar month.
  • Comply with state, local, and tribal licensing, regulatory, and tax laws.
  • Verify each jurisdiction's required forms and procedures directly — requirements can change.

Registering with ATF

  • Review the PACT Act Information Guide.
  • Complete ATF Form 5070.1 (and continuation sheet ATF Form 5070.1A, if needed).
  • Email the form(s) to PACTActregistrationinbox@atf.gov, or mail to the address at right.
